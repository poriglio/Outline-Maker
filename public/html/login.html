Welcome to Outlines, a tool for writers. Organize your stories into parts, chapters, and events, and move plot points around with the click of a button. Planning your novel has never been easier!

Outlines is still in development, so your are highly encouraged to keep a backup copy of your work. You can easily do this by clicking "File" at the top of the screen, then "Save page as..."

Also, make sure to click the "save" button to save your work! This will not happen automatically.
