PlotHound is a tool for writers to plan and organize their stories. Rearrange chapters and plot points with the click of a button.

PlotHound is still in development, so your are highly encouraged to keep a backup copy of your work. You can easily do this by clicking "File" at the top of the screen, then "Save page as..."

Also, make sure to click the "save" button to save your work! This will not happen automatically.
